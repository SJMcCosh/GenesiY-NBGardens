Frequently Asked Questions


Where's my order?

You can check the status of any orders in your Account.

If you still have questions, please Contact Us.


How can I return unwanted items?

Every order contains a freepost box that you can return unwanted/damaged items.

If yours is missing, please Contact Us.


How long will my order take?

We aim to have your items with you within 7 days.

Orders are dispatched every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday.


If after 7 days you have still not received your order, please Contact Us.

Where do you dispatch to?

We deliver to all UK addresses, including Northern Ireland.


At present, we cannot offer international delivery. Sorry.

How does "buy now, pay when satisfied" work?

It's all in the name. Once you receive you order, you have 28 days to decide if you're happy with it.

If it doesn't meet your needs, return it with the provided freepost box and you won't be charged.
